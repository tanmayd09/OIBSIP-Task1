Cakes n Craft ‘Baking Your Imagination’ – Our tag line speaks our mind. We bake what your mind speaks and imagines.

Cakes n Craft is a fine cake boutique that offers a vast array of exquisite desserts from macaroons, cupcakes, and pastries to the designer and handcrafted cakes in Indore. Being Central India’s Best Cake Shop and best known Patisserie, Cakes n Craft is known for its quality products and providing customer delight. CnC invites all to traverse through the cake paradise for further browsing of a collection of designer-crafted culinary products, premium macaroons, gift hampers, chocolate-filled savouries and a lot more.
